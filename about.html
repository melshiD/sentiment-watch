Sentiment Watch

Sentiment Watch (codename: *The Out Trap*) is a lightweight observatory for social pulse-checking, using publicly available Reddit data to track trends, detect shifts in sentiment, and surface topics with high emotional charge in hopes of building empathy rather and rage.

This tool interacts with Reddit via approved API endpoints and (in compliance with Reddit's terms) uses read-only, rate-limited access to aggregate public conversations. The purpose is simple: transparency into the voice of the crowd, without contributing noise.

No influence, just insight.
